Our compression technology marks a breakthrough by enabling the compression of any data, including those already compressed, thereby drastically reducing storage requirements and speeding up transmission rates. This advancement not only enhances storage efficiency across multiple memory tiers, enabling quicker data retrieval and lower latency, but also boosts performance across diverse domains such as System-on-Chip, High-Performance Computing, Artificial Intelligence, and more. SPIRAL-BOX (ready to use)

Our technology revolutionizes data storage and transfer, drastically reducing costs associated with storage infrastructure and energy consumption while optimizing bandwidth usage for high-speed data transfers. While classical algorithms barely achieve a compression ratio of 4:1 at best, reducing a 1 TB file to 250 GB, with SPIRAL-BOX, this ratio skyrockets to over 3000:1, compressing the same 1 TB down to approximately 0.3 GB. This signifies a paradigm shift in efficiency and the ability to handle large volumes of data more economically and swiftly than ever before, both in storage and transfer.
