SPIRAL-BOX (ready to use)

Our technology revolutionizes not only data storage but also its transfer, drastically reducing costs associated with storage infrastructure and energy consumption while optimizing bandwidth usage for high-speed data transfers. While classical algorithms barely achieve a compression ratio of 4:1 at best, reducing a 1 TB file to 250 GB, with SPIRAL-BOX, this ratio skyrockets to over 3000:1, compressing the same 1 TB down to approximately 0.3 GB. This signifies a paradigm shift in efficiency and the ability to handle large volumes of data more economically and swiftly than ever before, both in storage and transfer.

SPIRAL-CACHE (upcoming years)

Current microprocessors are facing increasing pressure in terms of memory capacity and effective bandwidth, particularly with the shift towards multi-core solutions. Amdahl's law, which dictates that computational performance requires proportional increases in memory capacity and effective bandwidth, has become a current bottleneck and a major issue. To address this challenge, SPIRAL-CACHE emerges as a solution by providing significantly improved effective bandwidth, benefiting both general-purpose processors and SoC accelerators in fields such as High-Performance Computing and Artificial intelligence.
