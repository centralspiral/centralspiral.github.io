Today’s data problem

  • Scale: petabytes across clusters/regions; backup windows slip and restores lag.
  • Budget: capacity growth and cross-region egress push costs up on-prem and in cloud.
  • Energy: every byte burns power; efficiency targets keep tightening.

Why existing approaches fall short

  • Classical lossless codecs (ZIP/LZ4/Zstd) hit limits on high-entropy logs, binaries, and floats.
  • Lossy methods change the payload — unacceptable for backup, audit, and science.
  • Dedup/tiering reorganize data but rarely shrink the bytes that must actually move.
