Empowering progress through data compression


At CENTRAL SPIRAL, we lead the digital revolution with our patented lossless data compression technology, designed to transform data storage and transmission. Our solution compresses all types of data, including pre-compressed files, significantly reducing storage needs and speeding up transmission rates. This improves storage efficiency across various memory tiers and enables faster data retrieval with minimal latency, addressing the challenges of bandwidth limitations.


Our technology provides a vital solution to the global bandwidth issue, ensuring optimal performance in industries such as SoC, HPC, AI, telecommunications, streaming, gaming and satellite communications. From XR and cloud computing to advanced medical applications, CENTRAL SPIRAL enhances performance and sustainability in data centers and microchips worldwide.


Our team, with over 12 years of experience in quantum physics, cryptography, computer science, electrical engineering, and remote sensing, has developed technologies for institutions like NASA, Fermilab, CERN, and leading universities. We specialize in quantum key distribution (QKD), high-performance computing (HPC), and lossless data compression, with patents in these areas. Our expertise also includes data analysis using LiDAR and satellites, driving innovation across multiple sectors. With over 25 scientific publications and 2,800 citations.
